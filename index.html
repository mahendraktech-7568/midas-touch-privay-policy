To create your account, we ask you to provide basic registration details, including a username and password. ◆3. Account Verification

New accounts are not active immediately. After registration, your account is reviewed and verified by our admin team. Once approved, you can log in using your username and password to view current stone rates, which are updated periodically within the App.

Your account information, including your password, is stored securely. We follow reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

◆7. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active, or as long as needed to provide you the App's services. You may request deletion of your data at any time as described below.

◆8. Account & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us using the details below. We will process verified deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe and confirm once your data has been removed.
